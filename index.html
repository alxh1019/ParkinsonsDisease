Indicators of Parkinson's Disease

Overall Introduction

Parkinson's disease affects millions worldwide, often going undetected for years before visible motor symptoms appear. In this webpage, data of motor indicators of Parkinson's disease via existing studies compile data visualizations in hopes to guide users in understanding this disease.

Data Introduction

This project integrates multiple datasets to provide a comprehensive view of the indicators of Parkinson's Disease. Below is an overview of the data sources used in our analysis.

1. Voice Inflections of Parkinson's Patients

Source: UC Irvine Machine Learning Repository

Description: Average / Minimum / Maximum Vocal Fundamental Frequency and Percentage of Jitters / Shimmers in Healthy and Parkinson's Patients.

  • Size: 195 Voice Recordings
  • Relevant Variables: MDVP:Fo(Hz), MDVP:Fhi(Hz), MDVP:Flo(Hz), MDVP:Jitter, MDVP:Shimmer, status
